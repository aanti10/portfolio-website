Beyond The Wishing Well

A film that takes viewers through the experiences of several people who have all been impacted by death. We are sharing their stories in the hopes that viewers will think more about both death and the value of life.

This was the first documentary I have ever worked on, and I learned a lot about the steps to making these kinds of projects. In this film I was the Cinematographer, Assistant Editor, and Colorist.
