Chimera

"Chimera is about a man who has a hard time dealing with his present and wants to keep reminiscing on his past. This film deals with themes of love and guilt, and the things we would do if we had too much power."
-Director Jacob Rodier's artist's statement

This was my first role as a cinematographer in a film, and I am still very proud of how it turned out. I was also the colorist.
